## 6. Original contributions of major significance

### 6.1 SpatialDINO (2024–2025)

- **What is novel:**
  - First 3D self-supervised vision transformer for cryo-electron tomograms.
  - Adapts DINO-style student/teacher contrastive learning into volumetric 3D — prior 3D ViTs were either supervised, sliced 2D, or used positional embeddings unsuitable for the cryo-ET geometry.
  - Custom 3D transformations replacing z-plane slicing — preserves spatial coherence in the latent representation.
  - Pretrained on **unannotated tomograms**; fine-tuned on a small labeled set.
- **Why it matters:** Beat the prior SOTA — including a method co-led by **Nobel laureate Eric Betzig** — on downstream subcellular structure prediction. Demonstrates that for biological imaging at near-atomic resolution, scale-efficient self-supervised pretraining beats hand-engineered priors.
- **External impact:**
  - BioRxiv preprint indexed and cited.
  - Generates derivative work in the Kirchhausen Lab program (the two _Journal of Cell Biology_ follow-on papers in §3).
  - PyTorch upstream contribution (see §6.2) was a direct byproduct.

### 6.2 PyTorch open-source contribution — issue [#144779](https://github.com/pytorch/pytorch/issues/144779)

- **What it is:** Diagnosed and reported a Rendezvous (RDZV) backend issue affecting Infiniband-backed multi-node distributed training during SpatialDINO scale-out.
- **Significance:** PyTorch is the dominant deep-learning framework — this is a contribution to the **infrastructure that the entire field uses**. The issue describes a specific failure mode that affects any sufficiently large multi-node training job using IB.
- **Documentation:** <https://github.com/pytorch/pytorch/issues/144779>

### 6.3 Dynamic Checkpointing in Apache Flink (2024 — BU Master's thesis)

- **What is novel:** Adaptive checkpoint cadence driven by live backpressure signals. Static checkpoint intervals are a tax in idle periods and a stall during bursts; my controller measures per-operator backpressure and tunes cadence dynamically.
- **External relevance:** Apache Flink is the foundation of streaming workloads at LinkedIn, Alibaba, Uber, and Stripe. Backpressure-aware checkpointing is a generalizable technique; the thesis benchmarks it on **NEXMARK**, the standard streaming benchmark.

### 6.4 Benmore Foundry CLI (2025–present)

- **What it is:** Internal orchestration layer for SMB AI consulting engagements at Benmore Technologies.
- **Originality:** One of the **first commercially-deployed Claude Code orchestration systems** (Anthropic's agentic CLI). Foundry abstracts over Claude Code's skill system, hooks, and MCP servers to enable a **forward-deployed engineering practice** that scales with one operator per ~5 client engagements.
- **External impact:** Drove Benmore's revenue from $150k cumulative to $150k every 15 days (a documented operating result). Adopted internally as the org's primary engagement substrate.
